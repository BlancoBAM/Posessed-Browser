---
title: "Update on macOS"
description: "How to update Posessed Browser on macOS"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your version">
    Go to **Settings > About Posessed Browser** to see your current version.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/about-browseros.png" alt="Settings page showing About Posessed Browser section" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for auto-update">
    Posessed Browser on macOS updates automatically. Restart your browser to apply pending updates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manual download (if needed)">
    If auto-update isn't working, download from [github.com/BlancoBAM/Posessed-Browser/releases](https://github.com/BlancoBAM/Posessed-Browser/releases) or [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/BlancoBAM/Posessed-Browser/releases).
  </Step>
</Steps>
